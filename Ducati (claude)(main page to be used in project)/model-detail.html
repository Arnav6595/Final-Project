Key Features

Desmosedici Stradale Engine

The 1,103 cc Desmosedici Stradale engine is the closest thing to a MotoGP engine available on a production bike. Its counter-rotating crankshaft and Twin Pulse firing order make it unique in the motorcycle world, delivering extraordinary performance.

Advanced Electronics Package

The latest-generation electronics package based on a 6-axis Inertial Measurement Unit includes: ABS Cornering EVO, Ducati Traction Control EVO 3, Ducati Slide Control, Ducati Wheelie Control EVO, Ducati Power Launch, Ducati Quick Shift up/down EVO 2, and Engine Brake Control EVO.

Aerodynamic Efficiency

The design incorporates MotoGP-derived aerodynamic winglets that produce high downforce, enhancing braking stability and reducing wheelie tendency. The fairings have been meticulously shaped to maximize cooling and aerodynamic efficiency.

Technical Specifications

Engine

Type Desmosedici Stradale 90° V4, counter-rotating crankshaft, 4 Desmodromic timing, 4 valves per cylinder
Displacement 1,103 cc
Bore x Stroke 81 x 53.5 mm
Compression Ratio 14.0:1
Power 214 HP (157.5 kW) @ 13,000 rpm
Torque 124 Nm (12.6 kgm) @ 9,500 rpm
Fuel System Electronic fuel injection system. Twin injectors per cylinder. Full ride-by-wire elliptical throttle bodies
Exhaust 4-2-1-2 system with 2 catalytic converters and 2 lambda probes

Transmission

Gearbox 6-speed with Ducati Quick Shift up/down EVO 2
Primary Drive Straight cut gears; Ratio 1.80:1
Ratio 1=38/14 2=36/17 3=33/19 4=32/21 5=30/22 6=30/24
Final Drive Chain; Front sprocket 16; Rear sprocket 41
Clutch Hydraulically controlled slipper and self-servo wet multiplate clutch

Chassis

Frame Aluminum alloy "Front Frame"
Front Suspension Öhlins NIX30 43 mm fully adjustable fork with TiN treatment
Front Wheel 5-spoke forged aluminum alloy wheel 3.50" x 17"
Front Tire Pirelli Diablo Supercorsa SP 120/70 ZR17
Rear Suspension Fully adjustable Öhlins TTX36 unit. Electronic compression and rebound damping adjustment
Rear Wheel 5-spoke forged aluminum alloy wheel 6.00" x 17"
Rear Tire Pirelli Diablo Supercorsa SP 200/60 ZR17
